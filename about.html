This is me!

My name is Heather Stevens and I'm a front-end web developer.

I take a design centric approach to my websites thanks to my interests in the fine arts and design. While in university I focused on filmmaking and photography, but upon taking a course in web design I realized that I really enjoyed writing code and seeing what I could make from it. Since then I have taken art classes and spent a few years doing administrative work for a non-profit which has given me great organizational and people skills, but now I'm ready to get back to my true calling.

My knowledge encompasses HTML5, CSS3, and a modest amount of Javascript but I am always learning. I also have certifications in Google Analytics and Google Adwords and have a foundation of digital marketing knowledge that will help me create a site that is primed for SEO and marketing your business.

Check out my work on my portfolio or if you'd like to hire me, please contact me. And of course, have a wonderful day!
